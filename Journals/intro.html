Intro

My name is Morgan Thompson. I investigate the paranormal in my hometown with my friends.
I am starting this journal as a sort of second brain. A place to dump things from investigations, my own thoughts, information I find, etc. My own personal blog and journal.
This is a way to keep myself organized. Maybe I can have a whole index about all the things we’ve encountered.
There’s a lot to do, but I enjoy this kind of stuff.
